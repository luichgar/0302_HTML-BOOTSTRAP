Prepare your ROM library

Another important aspect of retro gaming is to build your ROM library. Here is a general list of systems that can be played on Android. Its important to be noted that not all systems will run at playable speeds this will depend on your phone processor performance or the emulation madurity so performance may vary. For PS2 and GameCube on lower-end Android devices, I recommend getting PAL region ROMs, because they cap out at 50Hz which will give you better performance than struggling to reach the standard 60Hz found in NTSC region ROMs.
